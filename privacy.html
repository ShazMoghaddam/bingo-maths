Privacy Policy

Bingo Maths! · Last updated: March 2026 · Developer: Shaz Moghaddam

Overview

Bingo Maths! is a times tables bingo game designed for children. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information. The short version: we collect nothing.

Data Collection

Bingo Maths! does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any personal data of any kind. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any information about the user or their device

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only, using the browser's localStorage API. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your high score and game scores
  • XP points and level progress
  • Earned badges
  • Game history (last 20 games)
  • Daily challenge completion status

This data can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app data in device settings.

Children's Privacy

Bingo Maths! is specifically designed for children aged 5 and above. In full compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations, we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. No account creation is required or possible. No internet connection is required to play.

Third-Party Services

Bingo Maths! does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or any other third-party services that could collect data.

The app uses Google Fonts (Righteous and Nunito) which are loaded from Google's CDN when an internet connection is available. This is a standard web font request; please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles font requests.

Advertisements

Bingo Maths! contains no advertisements of any kind.

In-App Purchases

Bingo Maths! contains no in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
